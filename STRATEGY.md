# AI Industry Strategy Notes

## The Core Tension at Google
Defending $280B search-ad revenue while 
transitioning to AI-first products.
Every AI feature that answers directly = 
potential ad revenue at risk.

## The Opportunity This Creates For PMs
This is not a technology problem.
It is a product and business model problem.
Great PMs who understand both sides of this 
tension are disproportionately valuable.

## The 5 Battles (My View)
1. Context Window — Google leads
2. Multimodal — Google has data advantage
3. Agents — Too early to call, highest stakes
4. Developer ecosystem — OpenAI leads mindshare
5. Hardware — NVIDIA dominates, everyone scrambling

## My Thesis
The agent war will define the next 5 years.
Security is the first enterprise vertical where 
agents will be deployed at scale.
That is the intersection I am building in.
